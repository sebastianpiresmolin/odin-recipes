High rib burger with sweet potato fries

Who doesn't love a tasty and juicy burger with sweet potato fries? So simple, yet so good. And it feels extra good to have made it yourself at home in the kitchen. Just to enjoy, from start to finish.

  • 1 fresh sweet potato strips (each 350 g)
  • 1 tablespoon oil/li>
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 1 dl smear
  • 2 table spoonsketchup
  • 1 steak tomato
  • 1/2 leaves of romain lettuce
  • 1 pack of prime rib hamburgers
  • 2 buns, preferably sourdough
  • 1 pack fo fresh sweet potato fries
  1. Set the oven to 225Â°C.
  2. Mix the sweet potatoes with half the oil and season with salt and pepper. Pour out onto a baking sheet and place in the oven on high for about 15 minutes.
  3. Mix the sour cream and ketchup. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Cut the tomato into slices and the romaine lettuce into strips.
  5. Fry the burgers in the remaining oil in a frying pan according to the instructions on the package and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve the fries with the burgers in the bread together with salad,dressing and tomato.

Classic Lasagna

Classic Lasagna sure is a dish you never grow tired off! But with this recipe your lasagna will be perfect with a soft and lovely texture, but also with a heavenly taste! The parmesan cheese is the dot on the i.

Ingredients

  • 2 yellow onions
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 table spoon cooking oil (olive oil, preferrably)
  • 4 table spoons tomato puree
  • 1 tea spoon dried thyme
  • 1 tea spoon dried rosemary
  • 500g crushed tomatoes
  • 1 meat broth cube
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 9 lasagna plates

Sauce

  • 6 table spoons butter
  • 6 table spoons wheat flour
  • 1 litre milk
  • 2 dl shredded parmesan

Steps

  1. Put oven on 175Â°C.
  2. Peel and chop the onion and garlic. Fry the beef, onions and garlic in a pan with oil. Add pureed tomatoes and let it fry with the beef. Season with thyme and rosemary. Add chrushed tomatoes and broth and let boil for 10 minutes. Taste with salt and pepper.
